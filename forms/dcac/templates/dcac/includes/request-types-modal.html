Request Types

The JCR wishes to ensure that the current reimbursement system for club and society funding does not put any students into financial difficulty, or prevent clubs and societies from being able to make larger purchases.

Therefore, the reimbursement form has been amended so that, in cases where a purchase presents itself as a financial difficulty, you may select the 'Large' option and place a request (with all the necessary information) so that college can complete the purchase for your society using a college credit card. The given amount will then be deducted from your society/club budget.
